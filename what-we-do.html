Advocacy and Campaigning

It is part of our major activities campaigning and advocating amongst the women populace the needs to know what women rights are and how to apply them in any given society.

Donate Now

Ending violence against women

Female genders world over are experiencing violence both physically, sexually and otherwise. In our efforts as women of knowledge, we are putting all the necessary forces to bring to an end such violence in our societies.

Donate Now

Economic empowerment

With the support we are getting from the citizens, we are empowering women with some incentives and encouraging them to start business no matter how small and to make a judicious use of such money given. In some cases, we train them and supply them with necessary tools.

Donate Now

Education Advancement

Girl child education in Nigeria, especially in the northern part, is majorly being neglected. As such, our organisation is empowering parents and encouraging them to enrol their daughters in Schools. We sometimes give scholarship to some selected smart ones amongst them

Donate Now

Poverty Relief

It is part of our poverty alleviation campaign soliciting for support and assistance from government and citizens for the welfarism of women in abject poverty.

Donate Now

Peace and Security

Our Organization is preaching peace and security amongst women populace in both Urban and Rural areas.

Donate Now

Humanitarian action

Our Organisation embarks in supplying food and sanitary pads to women, especially to those living in rural areas, as well as enlightening them on how to use them.

Donate Now

Women and girls with disabilities

In our efforts for societal inclusion, we designed a special package for women and girls living with disabilities. This special package is mainly targeted those living in rural areas.

Donate Now

Governance and National Planning

We enlighten women on governance and National Planning.
