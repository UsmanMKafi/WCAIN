About Us Step Forward Serve The Humanity Reach Out & Help

Women Capacity and Advocacy Initiative (WCAIN) is a feminist and women led, membership movement-support organization working to achieve gender justice and women's human rights in Nigeria and worldwide. The organization is aimed at empowering women, raising the standards of living for rural women and their families, the promoting of girls education using the strategic tools of advocacy, campaigns, empowerment and engagement to ensure equal participation of women and girls in nation building.
