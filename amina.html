Read More Amina Musa

Amina Musa is the Co-Founder of Women Capacity ad Advocacy Initiative, an organization she co-started in 2015 after conducting research at the U.S Presidents Emergency Plan for AIDS Relief (PEPFAR) on the right of women in accessing HIV/AIDS services at remote clinics.

She Co-Founded Women Capaciity and Advocacy Initiative to agitate and enhance effectiveness in accessing healthcare services in remote clinics. Having a background in impact evaluation and field research, Amina understood how to bring policymakers, development researchers, and NGO partners together to create next-generation Gender programming. Amina had worked with Institute of Human Virology Nigeria and has consulted with various International Organization. She has over fourteen (14) years of International experience in development intervention.
